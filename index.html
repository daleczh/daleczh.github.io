Research Interests

I am working on various topics in the interplay of computer science and economics, also known as algorithmic game theory.

I am, in particularly, interested in the complicated interaction between the servicing platform and served targets (e.g., advertising platform v.s. advertisers, e-commerce platform v.s. sellers, platform v.s. users, etc.), mainly in the following aspects:

  • How can the platform design algorithms to reach its goal or help targets reach their goals?
  • How can targets conversely exploit the platform's algorithm to better benefit themselves?
  • How can one simultaneously benefit the platform and their targets?
