Research Interests

I am much interested in the interplay of computer science and economics, also known as algorithmic game theory. More specifically, I am currently working on various problems in ad auctions. Previously, I have worked on the scalability issue of blockchain, when I mainly focused on the application of game theory and mechanism design in the field of blockchain.
